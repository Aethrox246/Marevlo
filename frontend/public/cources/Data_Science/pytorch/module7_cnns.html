1.ConvolutionIntuitionandReceptiveField

WhatisaConvolution?

Aconvolutionslidesasmallkernel(filter)overaninputimage.Ateachposition,itcomputes adotproductbetweenthekernelandthelocalpatchofpixels—producingafeaturemap thathighlightswhereverthatpatternappears.

Real-WorldAnalogy:AFlashlightScanningaDocument

Imagine shining a small flashlight across a page and noting how bright each patch is relative to a template.Ahorizontal-edgekernellightsupwhereverhorizontaledgesappear.Avertical-edge kernellightsupwhereverticaledgesappear.Stackenoughkernelsandyoudetecttextures, shapes,andeventuallyobjects.

classResidualBlock(nn.Module):
"""
AbasicResNetblock:F(x)+x
ThenetworklearnstheRESIDUAL(thedifference)ratherthanthefullmapping.

"""

def__init__(self,channels:int):
super().__init__()
self.block=nn.Sequential(
nn.Conv2d(channels,channels,3,padding=1,bias=False), nn.BatchNorm2d(channels),
nn.ReLU(),
nn.Conv2d(channels,channels,3,padding=1,bias=False), nn.BatchNorm2d(channels),
)
self.relu=nn.ReLU()

defforward(self,x):

returnself.relu(self.block(x)+x)

4.TransferLearning:FreezingandUnfreezing

TheCoreIdea

TrainingaCNNfromscratchonsmalldatasetsalmostalwaysfails.Instead:

1.Startwithapretrainedmodel(ImageNet-trainedResNet,EfficientNet,etc.) 2.Freezethebackbone—don’tupdatethefeatureextractorweights.

3.Trainonlytheclassificationhead—fast,data-efficient.

4.Fine-tune(optional):unfreezedeeperlayersandtrainend-to-endwithasmallLR.

defclassification_metrics(logits:torch.Tensor,targets:torch.Tensor, n_classes:int)->dict:
"""Per-classprecision,recall,F1andmacro/weightedaverages.""" preds=logits.argmax(dim=1)
metrics={}

8

per_class_f1=[]
forcinrange(n_classes):
tp=((preds==c)&(targets==c)).sum().float() fp=((preds==c)&(targets!=c)).sum().float() fn=((preds!=c)&(targets==c)).sum().float()

p

=tp/(tp+fp+1e-8)

r

=tp/(tp+fn+1e-8)

f1=2*p*r/(p+r+1e-8)
per_class_f1.append(f1.item())
metrics[f'class_{c}_f1']=f1.item()

metrics['accuracy']

=(preds==targets).float().mean().item()

metrics['macro_f1']

=np.mean(per_class_f1)

topk_preds=logits.topk(top_k,dim=1).indices

metrics[f'top{top_k}_acc']=topk_preds.eq(targets.unsqueeze(1)).any(dim=1).float().mean

returnmetrics
