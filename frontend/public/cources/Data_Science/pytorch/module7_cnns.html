1.ConvolutionIntuitionandReceptiveField

WhatisaConvolution?

Aconvolutionslidesasmallkernel(filter)overaninputimage.Ateachposition,itcomputes adotproductbetweenthekernelandthelocalpatchofpixels—producingafeaturemap thathighlightswhereverthatpatternappears.

Real-WorldAnalogy:AFlashlightScanningaDocument

Imagine shining a small flashlight across a page and noting how bright each patch is relative to a template.Ahorizontal-edgekernellightsupwhereverhorizontaledgesappear.Avertical-edge kernellightsupwhereverticaledgesappear.Stackenoughkernelsandyoudetecttextures, shapes,andeventuallyobjects.

4.TransferLearning:FreezingandUnfreezing

TheCoreIdea

TrainingaCNNfromscratchonsmalldatasetsalmostalwaysfails.Instead:

1.Startwithapretrainedmodel(ImageNet-trainedResNet,EfficientNet,etc.) 2.Freezethebackbone—don’tupdatethefeatureextractorweights.

3.Trainonlytheclassificationhead—fast,data-efficient.

4.Fine-tune(optional):unfreezedeeperlayersandtrainend-to-endwithasmallLR.
