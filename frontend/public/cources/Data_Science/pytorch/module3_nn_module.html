1.nn.Module,Parameters,andBuffers

Whatisnn.Module?

nn.ModuleisthebaseclassforeveryneuralnetworkinPyTorch.Itgivesyourmodelfour superpowers:

1.Parametertracking—anynn.Parameterorsub-moduleyouassignisautomatically registeredandreturnedby.parameters().

2.Statesaving/loading—.state_dict()and.load_state_dict()letyoucheckpoint andresume.

3.Devicemanagement—.to(device)movesallparametersandbuffersatonce. 4.Training/evalmodes—.train()and.eval()togglebehaviourofdropout,batch- norm,etc.

Real-WorldAnalogy:ABlueprintvsaBuilding

Aclassthatinheritsfromnn.Moduleislikeablueprint.Whenyouinstantiateit,youget anactualbuilding(amodelobject)withrealrooms(parameters)andfixtures(buffers).The blueprintcanbeusedtoconstructasmanyindependentbuildingsasyouwant.

def__init__(self,in_dim,hidden_dim,out_dim): super().__init__()
self.fc1=nn.Linear(in_dim,hidden_dim) self.fc2=nn.Linear(hidden_dim,out_dim) self.act=nn.ReLU()

def__init__(self,dims:list):
super().__init__()
#MUSTusenn.ModuleList—plainPythonlistwon'tregisterparameters!

self.layers=nn.ModuleList([
nn.Linear(dims[i],dims[i+1])foriinrange(len(dims)-1) ])
self.act=nn.ReLU()

defforward(self,x):
fori,layerinenumerate(self.layers): x=layer(x)

ifi<len(self.layers)-1:

#Noactivationafterlastlayer

x=self.act(x)
returnx
