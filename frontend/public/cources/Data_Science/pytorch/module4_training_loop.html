1.TheCanonicalTrainingLoop

TheFourSteps—EveryTime,InOrder

foreachbatch:
1.optimizer.zero_grad()
2.output=model(X)
3.loss=criterion(output,y) 4.loss.backward()
5.optimizer.step()

←clearoldgradients
←forwardpass
←computeloss
←backwardpass(computegrads)←updateparameters

Thisneverchanges.Everytrainingloopyouwrite,ever,followsthisstructure.

Real-WorldAnalogy:AStudentSittingExams 1.Zerograd—forgetlastweek’sexamfeedback. 2.Forwardpass—sittheexam(makepredictions).

1

3.Loss—getyourscore(howwrongwereyou?).

4.Backwardpass—analysewhichanswerswerewrongandwhy. 5.Optimizerstep—adjustyourstudyapproachbasedonfeedback. Repeatuntiltheexamscoreisgood.
