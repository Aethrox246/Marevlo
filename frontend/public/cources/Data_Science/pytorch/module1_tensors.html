2.BroadcastingRules

WhatisBroadcasting?

BroadcastingletsPyTorchdoarithmeticontensorsofdifferentshapesbyvirtuallystretch-ingthesmallertensor—withoutactuallycopyingdatainmemory.It’swhatmakesvectorised codebothfastandreadable.

TheTwoBroadcastingRules

PyTorchchecksshapesright-to-left,dimensionbydimension.Twodimensionsarecompatible if:

1.Theyareequal,OR
2.Oneofthemisexactly1(thatdimensiongetsstretched)

Ifthetensorshavedifferentnumbersofdimensions,theshapewithfewerdimsispadded with1sontheleft.
