3.LearningRateFinder
TheLRfindersweepsthelearningratefromverysmalltoverylargeoverafewiterations, recordingtheloss.TheoptimalLRisjustbeforethelossstartsclimbing.

<python>

deflr_finder(model:nn.Module,X:torch.Tensor,y:torch.Tensor, criterion,start_lr:float=1e-7,end_lr:float=10.0, n_steps:int=100)->tuple: """ SweepsLRfromstart_lrtoend_lrovern_steps.
