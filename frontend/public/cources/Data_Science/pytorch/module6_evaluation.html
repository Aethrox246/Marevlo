3.Calibration,Thresholding,PR/ROC

WhyThresholdMatters

Thedefaultthresholdof0.5israrelyoptimalforimbalancedtasks.ShiftittotradePrecision forRecalldependingonbusinesscost.

•HighRecall,LowPrecision:catchmostfraud,butflagmanyfalsepositives(costly forcustomers).

•HighPrecision,LowRecall:onlyflaghigh-confidencefraud,misssomecases(costly forlosses).
