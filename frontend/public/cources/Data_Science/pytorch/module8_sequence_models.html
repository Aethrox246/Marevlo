2.LSTMandGRU:WhenTheyStillWin
TheLSTMCellIntuition
AnLSTMmaintainstwostates:-Cellstatec_t—long-termmemory(the“conveyorbelt”).-Hiddenstateh_t—short-termworkingmemory.

Threegatescontrolwhattokeep,forget,andwrite:

Forgetgate:f_t=(W_f·[h_{t-1},x_t]+b_f) Input gate:i_t=(W_i·[h_{t-1},x_t]+b_i) Outputgate:o_t=(W_o·[h_{t-1},x_t]+b_o)

←whattoforget
←whattowrite
←whattooutput

3.AttentionIntuition

TheCoreIdea

Insteadofcompressingtheentiresequenceintoafixed-sizehiddenstate,attentionletsthe modellookbackatanypositioninthesequencewhencomputingeachoutput.

Real-WorldAnalogy:Readingalogtodetectananomaly.Ratherthansummarisingall 1000eventsintoasinglevector,attentionletsthemodelsay:“Tounderstandevent850,let mefocusonevents10,200,and847—they’rethemostrelevantcontext.”

#Scaleddot-productattention—theheartoftheTransformer defscaled_dot_product_attention(Q,K,V,mask=None): """ #Scale Q:(B,heads,T,d_k) —queries K:(B,heads,T,d_k) —keys V:(B,heads,T,d_v) —values Returns:attendedvalues+attentionweights """ d_k =Q.shape[-1] scores=torch.matmul(Q,K.transpose(-2,-1))/(d_k**0.5)ifmaskisnotNone: scores=scores.masked_fill(mask==0,float('-inf')) weights=torch.softmax(scores,dim=-1) #(B,heads,T,T) output =torch.matmul(weights,V) #(B,heads,T,d_v) returnoutput,weights#Demo:4heads,seq_len=5,d_k=8 B,heads,T,d_k=2,4,5,8 Q=torch.randn(B,heads,T,d_k) K=torch.randn(B,heads,T,d_k) V=torch.randn(B,heads,T,d_k) out,weights=scaled_dot_product_attention(Q,K,V) print("Attentionoutputshape:",out.shape) #(2,4,5,8) print("Attentionweightsshape:",weights.shape)#(2,4,5,5)—eachtokenattendstoall print("Attentionweightssum(shouldbe1.0):",weights[0,0,0].sum().item())
